Selecting Times

Each time block is at an interval of 15 minutes. To select a range of time, click on your desired start block and drag down to your desired end block. You can also click on each time block individually, however, you may only select contiguous time blocks. To request for multiple time ranges, you will need to make multiple request submissions. Clicking on a selected block will clear the selection on that block while clicking clear will remove the entire selection. Once you are ready, click continue. The next step will verify your time and you will be able to reselect your time range if you find that was not what you wanted.
